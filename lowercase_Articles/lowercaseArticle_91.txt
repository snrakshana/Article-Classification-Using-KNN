university of kelaniya (uok) undergraduates were, last week, provided training at a workshop in soft skills development pertaining to professional cv writing and facing an interview.
it was organised by the uok’s career guidance unit, together with the department of statistics & computer science and statistics & computer science students’ association.
mr. senior manager- employer branding & engagement, pearson lanka (pvt) ltd, shehan kumar, was the resource person for the workshop. present on the occasion were career guidance unit director senior lecturer jayantha munasinghe, head of the department of statistics & computer science, dr. d. d.m. jayasundara, academic staff of the faculty of science and undergraduates.
