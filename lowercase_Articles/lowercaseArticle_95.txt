the ministry of education (moe) will recruit 1,918 grade iii principals for national and provincial schools, moe sources said.
examinations were held for the selections .
among those who sat the examination in february this year, 3,880 received high marks, and will be interviewed for the final selections by end july. “the appointments, to be given next month, will be approved by the moe and sent to the provinces, to prevent any objections from the provincial schools,” the source said.
the last batch of grade iii principals were recruited in 2016, when 3,900 were recruited.
the principals will also be used for administrative activities.  – dw
