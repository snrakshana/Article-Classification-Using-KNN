the us$50 million global tea promotion campaign is set to kick off in september this year, plantation industries minister navin dissanayake said.
he told a media briefing on thursday that the campaign will start in russia and then move to another 11 countries and is scheduled to be held over a period of three years.
the target markets are russia, ukraine, japan, china, the us, australia, turkey, saudi arabia, uae, iran, chile and germany.
