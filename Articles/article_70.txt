The meteorology department says that showers are expected today in the Western, Sabaragamuwa, and Central provinces and in the
districts of Galle and Matara.
Thundershowers could develop at several places in the Uva and Easteren provinces and in several places in Mullativu after 2.00pm.
The department spokesperson stated that increased wind speeds of 50-55kmph in the seas areas from Negombo to Mannar via Puttalam
and from Hambantota to Pottuvil can be expected.
