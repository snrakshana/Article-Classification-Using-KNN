The Commandant of the STF, Senior DIG MR Latif testied before the parliament select committee when it convened at the parliament
complex at 10 am today.
Senior DIG Latif is still testifying before the committee that probing the Easter Sunday bomb attacks.
In addition, former DIG NK Ilangakoon, a Retired Major General Lal Perera and Bodu Bala Sena CEO Dilantha Vithanage are scheduled to
appear before the committee today.
Meanwhile, senior DIG in charge of the CID, Ravi Seneviratne, director of the CID, SSP Shani Abeysekera, Director of the TID SP Waruna
Jayasundara and detective in charge of the principles and extremist investigations unit of the division, Tharanga Pathirana testied before
the select committee yesterday.
The special select committee also obtained a statement from head of State Intelligence Service Senior DIG Nilantha Jayawardena
regarding the Easter Sunday attack in a place outside the parliament
Speaking to our news team the Chairman of the committee Deputy Speaker Ananda Kumarasiri stated that he gave evidence before the
committee for 4 hours
