The Ministry of Education today announced the dates of the second term holidays for students of all government schools.
Accordingly all government schools will close for the second term holidays on August 01 while they are to reopen for the third term on September 02, 2019.
Meanwhile the 2019 Advanced Level Examination is scheduled to be held from August 05 to August 31 this year with a number of schools to be used as examination centers and paper marking centers.
