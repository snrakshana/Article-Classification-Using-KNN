Former general secretary of the SLFP Professor Rohana Lakshman Piyadasa says that the continuation of discussions on forming a new
alliance with the Podu Jana Peramuna was discussed during the central committee meeting held yesterday.
Expressing his views to our news team, he noted that discussions were held regarding the provincial council elections.
The central committee meeting commenced at the presidents ocial residence around 7 pm yesterday and concluded around 9 pm.
