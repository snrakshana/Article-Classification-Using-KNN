Bangladesh captain Tamim Iqbal won the toss and elected to bat first in the second one-day international against Sri Lanka at R. Premadasa Stadium in Colombo.
Sri Lanka leads the three-match series 1-0 after beating Bangladesh by 91 runs in Friday’s opening match. 
Bangladesh brought in left-arm spinner Taijul Islam for pacer Rubel Hossain in their only change from the opening defeat.
Sri Lanka replaced retried pacer Lasith Malinga with Isuru Udana and left out Thisara Perera to bring all-rounder Akila Dananjaya for their only other change. 
Sri Lanka: Dimuth Karunaratne (Ct), Kusal Perera, Avishka Fernando, Kusal Mendis, Angelo Mathews, Lahiru Thirimanne, Dhananjaya de Silva, Akila Dananjaya, Nuwan Pradeep, Isuru Udana Lahiru Kumara
Bangladesh: Tamim Iqbal (C), Soumya Sarkar, Mohammad Mithun, Mushfiqur Rahim, Mohammad Mahmudullah, Mosaddek Hossain, Sabbir Rahman, Mehedi Hasan, Taijul Islam, Shafiul Islam, Mustafizur Rahman
