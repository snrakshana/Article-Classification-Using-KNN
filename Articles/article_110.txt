An exhibition of Sri Lankan artists – paintings, drawings and installations commemorating past acts concealed in shame, curated by Prof. Chandraguptha Thenuwara will be held  from July 24- 31 from 10 a.m to 7 p.m. at the Lionel Wendt Gallery.
This unique exhibition of the creative expressions of ten talented Sri Lankans is presented by the Colombo Cultural Hub Trust  and affords an excellent opportunity for the public to appreciate the commemorating of these acts of shame, as an expression of unity and peace.
