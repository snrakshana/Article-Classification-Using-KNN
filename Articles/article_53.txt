Three persons - two women and one boy died, and five others were injured, when their Jaffna bound bus from Colombo collided head-on with a lorry between Medawachchiya and Anuradhapura on Sunday night at 11.28 p.m., police said yesterday. 
A lorry that was transporting onions from Jaffna hit the bus at a bend on the A 9 highway in Wahamalugollewa. 
The deceased, Seetha Sivam Sivandhi (53) Avandani Govia (30) and Nandan Sirithai (12), were residents of Jaffna. The injured were rushed to the Anuradhapura Teaching Hospital.
