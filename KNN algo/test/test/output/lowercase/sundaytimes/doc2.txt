2019 electoral register for public viewing from aug 23 the electoral register for 2019 will be displayed to the public from august 23, the elections commission (ec) said. accordingly, these registers would be displayed at the grama niladhari offices, divisional secretariats and district secretariats. names which have been included or excluded from the list would also be listed for public viewing. individuals whose names have not been included in the list despite qualifiying to do so will be able to make an appeal at these government offices, the ec stated.
